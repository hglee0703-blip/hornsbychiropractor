Services

Conditions we commonly treat

Care starts with assessment, clear explanation, and a treatment plan matched to your symptoms, movement, and daily needs.

Neck pain

Neck pain and stiffness

Neck pain may relate to joint stiffness, muscle tension, posture, headaches, work setup, or recent strain. Treatment focuses on improving movement, calming irritated tissues, and helping you understand what aggravates your symptoms.

Lower back pain

Lower back pain and disc-related discomfort

Lower back pain can affect sitting, bending, lifting, walking, and sleep. Assessment helps identify whether stiffness, disc irritation, muscle guarding, or movement habits are contributing, then care is tailored to your presentation.

Headaches

Headaches related to neck tension

Some headaches can be influenced by neck joints, muscle tension, posture, or sustained desk work. Treatment may include hands-on care, movement advice, and practical strategies to reduce repeated strain.

Posture and recovery

Posture, movement, and long-term recovery

Posture care is not about forcing one perfect position. It is about building better movement options, reducing repeated overload, and supporting habits that help your body feel more comfortable through work, exercise, and daily life.
